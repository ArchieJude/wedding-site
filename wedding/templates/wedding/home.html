一切的開始，就像所有偉大的愛情故事一樣——一個男人在網路上稱讚了一個女人的狗。在2020年疫情最嚴峻的時期，Archie以人類史上最高明的開場白滑進了Ariel的私訊：誇獎她的狗。從一開始，Ariel就覺得Archie與她遇過的任何人都不同；他的個性、興趣，以及他自由奔放的生活方式都深深吸引著她。

他們的第一次約會去了滑板場，主要是因為Archie非常想展示他的技術，並慷慨地表示要把他所知道的一切都教給Ariel。結果是：她本來就已經相當厲害，根本不需要他的指導。到了約會結束時，他們都悄悄地想到了同一件事：這個人不一樣。

接下來的發展與慢火細燉截然相反。他們幾乎每天都在一起，很快就形影不離，幾個月內就直接搬進了Milltown。

剛搬在一起的那幾年讓他們學到了很多，無論是作為個人還是作為伴侶。他們深入了解彼此，相互扶持成長，為彼此的事業加油打氣，並因為對動物的共同熱愛而緊密相連（Ariel有兩隻狗，Archie有兩隻貓，加上他們養的各種魚，讓他們的生活忙碌而快樂）。Ariel也成功地把Archie改造成了一個美食愛好者：他們剛認識時，他最喜歡的食物是漢堡，還以為所有壽司都一樣，但現在他最愛的是小籠包。也是在這段時間裡，他們各自去了一個對對方而言意義深重的地方：Archie去了台灣，那裡是Ariel媽媽的故鄉；Ariel則去了蒙古，那是Archie的祖國。

2024年他們搬到了丹佛，幾乎很少待在家裡。他們把時間填滿了戶外探險：夏天釣鱒魚、野外露營，冬天則上山滑雪。

2025年他們搬回了新澤西。那年12月，在一趟夏威夷之旅中，Archie決定是時候了。對他來說，婚姻感覺像是他們已經共同建立的生活的自然延續，這讓他感到無比期待。恰巧那時，旅途中所有人都在尋找寶貝貝殼——那是夏威夷群島上罕見而珍貴的發現。Archie決定找到一顆大的就是一個信號，雖然他不想把如此重要的事完全交給命運，所以他努力搜尋。最後，故事有了一個完全符合他們整段愛情的轉折——是Ariel找到了那顆大貝殼。於是他求婚了。對Ariel來說，這是毫不猶豫的「願意」。她無法想像自己的生活中沒有他，而且說真的，她在心裡已經憧憬了一段時間了。她最高興的是，這一天終於來了。

就這樣，兩個世界、無數條魚、一連串的冒險，以及一顆非常大的貝殼，共同促成了一場在台灣舉辦的婚禮。

It started, as all great romances do, with a man complimenting a woman's dogs on the internet. In the thick of the 2020 pandemic, Archie slid into Ariel's DMs with the smoothest opener known to humankind: praise for her dogs. From the start, Ariel felt like Archie was different from anyone she'd ever met; his personality, hobbies, and his free-spirited lifestyle all intrigued her.

For their first date, they went skateboarding, mostly because Archie really wanted to show off his skills and generously offered to teach Ariel everything he knew. Plot twist: she was already pretty good and didn't need much of his help at all. By the end of it, they'd both quietly landed on the same thought: this one's special.

What followed was the opposite of a slow burn. They hung out almost every day, got inseparable fast, and within a few months had skipped straight to moving in together in Milltown.

Those first few years living together taught them a lot, individually and as a couple. They learned each other inside and out, grew alongside one another, cheered on each other's careers, and bonded deeply over their shared love of animals (between Ariel's 2 dogs, Archie's 2 cats, and their multiple fish their hands were happily full). Ariel also successfully converted Archie into a foodie: when they first met, his favorite food was a burger and he thought all sushi tasted the same, but these days it's xiao long bao. It was also during this stretch that they each made the trip to a place close to the other's heart: Archie to Taiwan, where Ariel's mom is from, and Ariel to Mongolia, Archie's home country.

In 2024 they moved to Denver, where they were rarely home. They filled their time with outdoor adventures: trout fishing, camping in the summer, and snowboarding in the winter.

In 2025 they moved back to New Jersey. That December, on a trip to Hawaii, Archie decided it was time. For him, marriage felt like a natural continuation of the life they'd already built together, and that excited him. As it happened, everyone on the trip was hunting for cowrie shells, the rare and prized find of the islands. Archie decided that locating a big one would be a sign, though, not wanting to leave such an important matter entirely to destiny, he searched hard. In a final twist worthy of their whole story, it was Ariel who found the huge shell. So he proposed. For Ariel, it was an easy yes. She couldn't picture her life with anyone else, and honestly had been picturing it with Archie for a while now. Mostly, she was just glad it had finally happened.

And that's how two worlds, countless fish, a whole lot of adventures, and one very large seashell led to a wedding in Taiwan.
